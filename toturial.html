PERSONAL BUDGET TRACKER
TOTURIAL

1. Log in to your account first.

2. Fill up the budget form, ensuring that only numbers are entered.

3. Click the “Set Budget” button.

4. In the Expenses Form, type your Product Title or name; then put the amount of that product in the second container. Click the “Check Amount” button.

5. After filling up the form, you will now see how much your total budget, expenses, and balance are.

6. You will see the list of your expenses. Remember that you are allowed to change and delete items using the buttons on the left side of the Expenses List.
